Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Learn how NoSpyLinks protects your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information:

  • No Personal Data Processing: NoSpyLinks processes all URLs locally in your browser. We never receive, store, or process your URLs or email content on our servers.
  • Basic Analytics: We may collect anonymous usage statistics such as page views and general geographic location (country level) to improve our service.
  • Contact Information: When you contact us via email, we store your email address and message content solely for the purpose of responding to your inquiry.

2. How We Use Your Information

Any information we collect is used exclusively to:

  • Respond to your support requests and inquiries
  • Improve our website and services based on anonymous usage patterns
  • Send important service updates (only if you've contacted us previously)

3. Data Processing and Storage

Local Processing: All URL cleaning and processing happens entirely in your web browser. Your data never leaves your device when using our core service.

No Third-Party Tracking: We do not use third-party tracking scripts, social media pixels, or advertising networks that could compromise your privacy.

Secure Communications: All communications with our servers use HTTPS encryption.

4. Cookies and Local Storage

NoSpyLinks uses minimal local storage:

  • Theme Preference: We store your dark/light mode preference in your browser's local storage
  • No Tracking Cookies: We do not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes
  • Essential Functionality: Any storage is purely for improving your user experience

5. Third-Party Services

NoSpyLinks is designed to minimize third-party dependencies:

  • Hosting: Our website is hosted on secure, privacy-respecting infrastructure
  • Email: Contact form submissions are processed through our secure email system
  • No Analytics Tracking: We do not use Google Analytics or similar tracking services

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about any data we have about you
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your contact information from our records
  • Correction: Request correction of any inaccurate information
  • Portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format

7. Data Retention

  • Contact Information: We retain contact information for 2 years after your last communication with us
  • Usage Data: Anonymous usage statistics are retained for 1 year for service improvement purposes
  • User-Requested Deletion: We will delete your information within 30 days of a verified deletion request

8. Security Measures

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all communications
  • Secure server infrastructure with regular security updates
  • Limited access to any stored data
  • Regular security audits and monitoring

9. Children's Privacy

NoSpyLinks does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will delete such information.

10. International Users

NoSpyLinks is available globally. If you are using our service from outside the United States, please note that any information you provide may be transferred to and processed in the United States, where our servers are located.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. When we do:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • For significant changes, we will provide notice on our website
  • Your continued use of NoSpyLinks after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

  • Email: support@nospylinks.com
  • Subject: Privacy Policy Question

Commitment to Privacy: NoSpyLinks was created with privacy as a core principle. We believe in protecting your digital privacy and will continue to operate with transparency and minimal data collection.
