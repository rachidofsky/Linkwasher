NoSpyLinks is a simple, privacy-focused web tool that removes tracking parameters from URLs found in emails, social media posts, and other content. In today's digital world, almost every link you click contains tracking codes that monitor your behavior across the web.

Our tool helps you regain control over your privacy by stripping these unnecessary parameters while preserving the original functionality of the links.

üéØ The Problem We Solve

Modern marketing and social media platforms inject tracking parameters into virtually every link:

  • Email Marketing: utm_source, utm_medium, utm_campaign
  • Google Ads: gclid, yclid
  • Facebook/Meta: fbclid, igshid
  • HubSpot: _hsenc, _hsmi
  • MailChimp: mc_cid, mc_eid

These parameters allow companies to track your clicks, build profiles about your interests, and follow you across websites. NoSpyLinks removes these tracking codes while keeping the links functional.

NoSpyLinks uses advanced pattern matching to identify and remove tracking parameters while preserving the essential parts of your URLs:

1. URL Detection

Our tool scans your pasted content for URLs in multiple formats:

  • Plain text URLs (https://example.com/...)
  • HTML link attributes (href="..." and src="...")
  • Email forwarding formats

2. Parameter Analysis

We identify and remove known tracking parameters including:

  • UTM parameters (utm_source, utm_medium, etc.)
  • Click tracking IDs (gclid, fbclid, etc.)
  • Email platform trackers (_hsenc, mc_cid, etc.)
  • Social media trackers (igshid, si, etc.)

3. Clean Output

You get the same functional URLs without the privacy-invasive tracking codes.
