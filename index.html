    What is NoSpyLinks? Professional URL Tracking Parameter Removal

    NoSpyLinks is a free, privacy-focused web application designed to remove tracking parameters from URLs and email links. Whether you're dealing with utm_source, utm_medium, utm_campaign, gclid, fbclid, or other tracking codes, our tool instantly cleans your links while protecting your privacy.

    Supported Tracking Parameters

    Our advanced URL cleaner removes the following tracking parameters:

    • UTM Parameters: utm_source, utm_medium, utm_campaign, utm_term, utm_content
    • Google Analytics: gclid, gclsrc, _ga, _gl
    • Facebook: fbclid, mc_cid, mc_eid
    • Instagram: igshid, si
    • HubSpot: _hsenc, _hsmi
    • Other platforms: yclid, pk_campaign, pk_kwd

    Why Remove URL Tracking Parameters?

    🔒 Privacy Protection

    Prevent companies from tracking your browsing behavior across websites and building detailed profiles about your interests.

    🧹 Clean URLs

    Share clean, readable URLs without ugly tracking parameters that clutter links and make them hard to read.

    ⚡ Better Performance

    Shorter URLs load faster and are easier to manage, especially when sharing links in emails or social media.
