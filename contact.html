üöÄ Quick Help

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is EmailCleaner really private?

Yes! All processing happens locally in your browser. No data is sent to our servers or any third parties. You can even use EmailCleaner offline once the page is loaded.

Q: What tracking parameters does EmailCleaner remove?

We remove 15+ common tracking parameters including UTM parameters, Google Click IDs, Facebook tracking, email platform trackers, and social media tracking codes.

Q: Can I use EmailCleaner on mobile devices?

Absolutely! EmailCleaner is fully responsive and works great on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.

Q: Is there a browser extension available?

Currently, EmailCleaner is available as a web application. We're considering a browser extension for the future based on user feedback.

Q: How often is the tracking parameter list updated?

We regularly monitor new tracking methods and update our parameter list to stay current with the latest tracking techniques.
